Investment Banking · LBO · DCF · M&A UW Finance · Wisconsin School of Business
Monster Beverage Corporation · Full IB Model Suite
Built a comprehensive investment banking model suite on Monster Beverage (MNST) covering an integrated operating model, DCF valuation, LBO analysis, and precedent transactions. The DCF yielded an implied EV of $47.3B using an 8.05% WACC across multiple operating cases. The LBO was structured at a $63.6B entry EV (25.9x 2024A EBITDA) with a 7x total leverage package including bank debt and mezzanine, a 5-year hold, and a 15x exit multiple. The precedent transactions analysis covered 6 beverage M&A deals including Coca-Cola / Monster, PepsiCo / Rockstar, and KDP / GHOST.

M&A Strategy · Valuation · Investment Recommendation Group Case Presentation
Brown-Forman Acquisition Analysis · Strategic Valuation Case
Built and presented a 29-slide strategic analysis of a potential Brown-Forman acquisition following market reports of Pernod Ricard interest. The case evaluated industry consolidation, Brown-Forman's premium spirits platform, buyer universe, standalone valuation, DCF, LBO feasibility, M&A contribution analysis, synergy value, ROIC, comparable companies, and precedent transactions. The final recommendation was that Brown-Forman should remain independent unless offered a clearly compelling control premium that fully reflects its scarcity value, family voting control, and long-term bourbon/RTD growth exposure.
